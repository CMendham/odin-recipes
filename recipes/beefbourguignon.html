Paprikash

A delicous looking bowl of beef bourguignon.

Description:

Beef bourguignon is a savory and flavorful dish from Hungary. This delicous dish is made with beef, red wine gravy, and other spices.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 6 ounces of bacon chopped
  • 3 pounds of beef briscket cut into chunks
  • 1 large carrot sliced
  • 1 large white onion diced
  • 8 cloves of garlic minced
  • salt and pepper
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 10 pearl onions
  • 3 cups of your favorite red wine
  • 3 cups beef stock
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 beef bullion cube
  • 1 tablespoon chopped thyme
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
  • 2 bay leaves
  • mushrooms if you like mushrooms
  • 3 tablespoons butter

Steps:

  1. Heat oven to 350F.
  2. Saute bacon in oil in heavy pot and transfer to large dish.
  3. Sear the sides of beef and transfer to dish with bacon.
  4. Use remaining oil to saute carrots and white onions for four minutes, then add garlic for one minute, drain all but a tablespoon of oil and add in the meat. Season with salt and pepper, sprinkle with flour, mix and cook for 6 minutes.
  5. Add the pearl onions, wine and enough stock to just cover the meat. Add the tomato paste, bullion, and herbs.
  6. Once simmering, cover, trasnfer to oven and cook for 3 hours.
  7. Strain sauce into large pot, skim any fat,remove all herbs. Sauce should be thick enough to coat a fork lightly. If it's too thick,
  8. Dump meat and veggies back into the pot from the oven.
  9. Salt and pepper to taste, serve with your favorite pasta and pour the sauce over everything!
