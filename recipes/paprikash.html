Paprikash

A delicous looking bowl of paprikash.

Description:

Paprikash chicken is a popular dish from Hungary. This delicous dish is made with paprika, chicken, and other spices.

Ingredients:

  • 1x Whole Cut Chicken
  • 1x Onion
  • 2x Carrots
  • 1x Celery
  • 1x Bell Pepper
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Tomato Paste
  • Mustard
  • White Wine
  • 2x Bay Leaves
  • Olive Oil

Steps:

  1. Cook the chicken in a little olive oil and add some salt and pepper.
  2. Remove the chicken and set aside.
  3. Saute finly diced vegetables in the same pan.
  4. Add tomato paste and bay leaves and continue to cook for another five minutes when vegetables are soft from saute.
  5. Add the chicken, one tablespoon of paprika, and one cup of wine and mix all together.
  6. Cook for about an hour.
  7. Serve with your favorite pasta.
